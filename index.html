What is CBSS?

The Consulting & Business Strategy Society at NYU SPS provides a platform for students to explore careers in consulting through case competitions, skill-building events, alumni panels, and live projects with real-world clients.

Our Mission

CBSS aims to bridge academic learning with practical application while building a community of curious problem-solvers and innovative thinkers across NYU SPS.
